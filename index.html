Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our HDPE pipes.

What is HDPE pipe used for?

HDPE pipes are used widely in municipal water supply, industrial effluent disposal, agricultural irrigation, telecommunication ducting, and natural gas distribution due to their durability and flexibility.

What is the working pressure of HDPE pipes?

Our pipes support working pressures ranging from PN 2.5 up to PN 20, depending on the Standard Dimension Ratio (SDR) and application requirements.

Do you provide custom lengths?

Yes, we can manufacture custom lengths. We offer coils up to 1000 meters for smaller diameters and standard 6m/12m straight lengths for larger ones.

How are the pipes joined?

They are securely joined using butt fusion, electrofusion, or mechanical compression fittings, ensuring leak-free, high-strength connections.

Are these pipes UV resistant?

Our black HDPE pipes are treated with at least 2% well-dispersed carbon black, making them fully resistant to UV radiation for outdoor installations.
